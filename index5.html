How It Works

  1. Each participant picks 8 golfers across 4 tiers: 3 from Tier 1, 2 from Tier 2, 2 from Tier 3, 1 from Tier 4.
  2. Your best 4 of 8 scores count toward your team total.
  3. Lowest total wins.
  4. Golfers who miss the cut receive a score of 80 (par + 8) for each of Rounds 3 and 4.
  5. Tiebreaker: closest guess to the winning score without going over. If all go over, closest wins.
